App Features

Learn music remotely via a smart Android app.
Research on scientific and scalable way of teaching music

Play Metronome and Scale

User can use this feature to listen and record songs

Sing and Analyse

Students can give answers and get instant feedback in form of plot

Listen and Learn

Students can listen and sing in sync with teacher for better learning

Change Tonic and BPM

Change the tonic and bpm of the song in realtime as per your comfort

Indian Music Notations

Indian Music Notations can be used to describe the lessons.

Useful Tools

Tools like Violin Tuner can be used to tune your instrument.
